# Line Dance

A `line` connects two points. It needs 4 parameters:

- x1, y1: starting point
- x2, y2: ending point

---

## Task 1 — Single Line

Draw a diagonal line from top-left (0, 0) to bottom-right (400, 400).

Use `line(x1, y1, x2, y2)`.

**Validation:** `line(4)`

---

## Task 2 — Styled Line

Add `stroke()` and `strokeWeight()` to make the line thicker and colored.

Try `stroke(255, 100, 100)` and `strokeWeight(4)`.

**Validation:** `line(4)` + `strokeWeight(1)`
