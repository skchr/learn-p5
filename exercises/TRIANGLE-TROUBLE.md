# Triangle Trouble

A `triangle` needs 6 parameters — three (x, y) vertices:

- x1, y1: first vertex
- x2, y2: second vertex
- x3, y3: third vertex

---

## Task 1 — Basic Triangle

Draw an upward-pointing triangle with vertices at (200, 50), (100, 300), (300, 300).

Use `triangle(x1, y1, x2, y2, x3, y3)`.

**Validation:** `triangle(6)` + `pixelMatch(200, 250, [100, 255, 150])`
